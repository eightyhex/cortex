---
description: Triage your Cortex inbox — categorize and organize pending notes
---

Use the `mcp_process_inbox` tool to list all items in the user's Cortex inbox.

$ARGUMENTS

Present each item with its title, summary, suggested type/folder, suggested tags, and age. Help the user decide what to do with each item: promote to a proper note type, archive, or delete. Use `edit_note` or `mcp_create_note` to act on their decisions.
